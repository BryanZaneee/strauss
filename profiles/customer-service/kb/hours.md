## Holiday closures

We are closed on Thanksgiving Day and December 25.

We close early (3:00 PM) on December 24 and December 31, and on July 4.

## Weather closures

If we have to close for weather (snow, power outage), we post on our website and Instagram by 6:00 AM. If you're not sure whether we're open, the website is the source of truth.

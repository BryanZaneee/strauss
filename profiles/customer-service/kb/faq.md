# Frequently asked questions

## Do you have WiFi?

Yes. The network name and password are posted at the counter and on each table. We ask that laptops are closed during the busy 11 AM–1 PM weekday lunch window.

## Are you pet-friendly?

Yes — on the patio. Service animals are welcome inside. We keep a water bowl out back near the bike rack.

## Do you have oat milk?

Yes. We carry oat, almond, whole, and skim milk. There is no upcharge for non-dairy milk.

## Are your pastries gluten-free?

We have one rotating gluten-free pastry on Saturdays and Sundays (a tea cake or muffin from Rye & Sparrow). Our kitchen is not a dedicated gluten-free space — there is risk of cross-contact. If you have celiac disease or a serious wheat allergy, we recommend ordering only the sealed retail items (whole-bean coffee, packaged tea).

## Where can I park?

Street parking on Cedar Street is metered Monday through Saturday until 6 PM. There is a small free lot behind the shop accessible from the alley off 4th Avenue (8 spots, first-come). Bike rack out front.

## Do you sell gift cards?

Yes — physical cards at the counter (any amount from $10) and digital cards on our website (delivered by email).

## Do you take large orders or cater?

Yes. We need 24 hours' notice for catering. Email orders@lanternlane.example with the date, headcount, and what you'd like (coffee box, pastry box, sandwich platter). We'll send a confirmation and an invoice.

## Can I work / study here for a few hours?

Of course — that's part of why we exist. We just ask that during the busy 11 AM–1 PM weekday lunch rush, laptops stay closed and tables are shared if seating gets tight.

## Do you do loyalty cards or a rewards program?

Not currently. We're a small two-shop operation and chose to keep prices a bit lower instead of running a points program.

## Are you hiring?

Sometimes! Open positions are posted on our website's "Join the team" page. We don't accept walk-in resumes — please apply through the website link.
